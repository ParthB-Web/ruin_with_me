Ruin with Me

Tap lines to reveal
I want to use you.
Not like a stranger. Not like a toy.
But like something precious I get to ruin slowly.
I want to take all that innocence, all that softness you don’t even know you’re radiating—
and wrap it around my hunger.
You say you don’t know what you want.
You speak like a girl lost in fog—
but I see the way your breath catches when I get close.
The way your silence isn't absence—
it’s surrender you don’t know how to name.
So let me name it for you.
You want to be ruined.
Not hurt. Not humiliated.
You want to be torn open with reverence—
like a sacred text that only I’m allowed to read, defile, and worship all at once.
I want to drag you out of your confusion by the throat of your desire.
Not to punish you.
But to show you who the fuck you are when you stop hiding behind “I don’t know.”
I want to fuck you until you cry— not from pain,
but from release.
Until the weight of everything you’ve held in your body melts onto my skin.
I want to destroy your restraint.
Shatter every version of you that ever flinched, ever hesitated,
ever looked in the mirror and said, “I’m not that kind of girl.”
You are.
With me— you fucking are.
I want to fuck your mouth until your thoughts are gone.
I want to pin your wrists, not to control you—
but to make you feel what it’s like to stop carrying the burden of decision.
Let me be the voice that claims you.
That rips apart the nice-girl mask,
and replaces it with moans, bite marks, and the ruin of your old self.
I want you to collapse into the wreckage of who you thought you had to be.
And I’ll be right there.
Breathing with you.
Loving you in the ruin.
Because I’m not here for just your body.
I want to own your unspoken places.
Your fear. Your trust. Your fucking confusion.
And when I’m done—
when you're trembling, aching, covered in the wreck of us—
I’ll hold your face like you’re holy.
I’ll say,
“You’re safe.”
Because everything I destroy, I protect.
So, I’ll hold you there.
I’ll never walk away from what I ruin.
Because I won’t just ruin you for me—
I’ll ruin myself in the process.
And I’ll fucking love it.
You’re not a body to me. You’re a universe I want to tear through and taste every part of.
You already know how much I enjoy figuring out how things work,
I want to study your mind like a sacred text,
kiss the parts of your past no one’s asked about,
fuck your self-doubt out of existence.
And everything I take— I love.
Not politely. Not gently.
I love you violently, with the kind of hunger that says:
You’re mine now. And you’re free in that.
So let me ruin you— with care, with fire, with worship.
Let me fuck you into clarity.
And when you wake up from this—
you won’t be confused anymore.
And when we’re both unrecognizable—
I’ll still be here.
In the ashes.
Loving you deeper.
Always.
—Parth B.
